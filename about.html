Hey there!

Welcome to my little corner of the internet. I'm a graphic designer who's just moved to Melbourne, Australia from Sweden. On this site, you'll find some of my work from the past few years.

Currently, the areas my heart beats a bit faster for are branding, web design, and editorial design. I do, however, also enjoy mixing in some copywriting, UX design, and just pure nitty gritty fiddling around in the Adobe CC software. As a designer, I almost always strive for aesthetic functionality.

I got my Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design and Communication from Linköping University earlier this year, after having spent five semesters in beautiful Norrköping in Sweden, and one semester at RMIT here in Melbourne.

At the moment I'm open to taking on new projects, freelance or not. Hit me up using the contact form or shoot me a dm at @postersandtea if you wanna discuss working together in any capacity!
