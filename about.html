Hey there!

Welcome to my little corner of the internet. On this website, which I did indeed build myself from scratch (thank you for asking, it really was quite the journey), I've gathered some of my favourite pieces of mine from the past few years. Have a look around or keep reading to hear more about who I am.

I'm a recently graduated graphic designer who's just finding my footing in the industry. Having just spent a year in Australia working in printing, drinking boatloads of coffee and learning about footy, I'm now back in the mother country, ready to kick some ass further my career and soak up new knowledge like a sponge.

As a designer, the areas my heart beats a bit faster for are branding, editorial design, and marketing. I do, however, also enjoy mixing in some copywriting, UX design, and just pure nitty gritty fiddling around in the Adobe CC software.

I got my Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design and Communication from Linköping University in 2023, after having spent five semesters in Norrköping, and one semester at RMIT in Melbourne.

Want to get in touch? Find my contact details on the contact page.
