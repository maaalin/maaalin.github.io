Internship at Identx

What: Internship at communication agency Identx

When: 2022

Role: Graphic designer. Collaborated with the creatives and account execs at Identx

Objective: Everything from producing infographics to sketching logo ideas. As the internship was part of the course TNGD35 at Linköping University, it also included creating a poster about the internship.

Challenges: Adhering to the clients' wishes. Strived to be attentive and remade designs numerous times in order to do this. As for the poster, finding a functional yet fun way to organize the text was the biggest challenge. Changed courses midway through when the first one didn't work as planned, see sketches and final poster lower down on this page.
