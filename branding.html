Branding

What: Graphic identities for fictional brands

When: 2023

Client: Frames & Frames, a company selling glasses and picture frames / Creative Tub, a creative agency / Bernie's, a coffee shop

Target group: Young adults for Frames & Frames / SMEs for Creative Tub / Caffeine addicts and tea lovers for Bernie's

Objective: To develop graphic identities for these brands

Challenges: Finding a unique expression for each brand, as well as pushing myself out of my comfort zone. In order to do this, a lot of time has been spent looking for inspiration, trying out different expressions, and failing.
