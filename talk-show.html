What's Poppin'?

What's Poppin'? was a livestreamed talk show for the course Live Media at RMIT University (2022). This satirical news show had a target group consisting of young adults, and part of the assignment was to create graphics for it. I volunteered to do it, and while I was at it decided to give the show a proper graphic profile. On this page you'll find the results of that endeavour.
