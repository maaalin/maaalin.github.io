The Poster Project

September 2023 I decided to put my down time at work to use and make a poster every day for a month or two or three or more. I ended up doing this for 100-and-something days, and it's been a design project like no other. Below are the posters in chronological order, and I think not only can you see the journey from doing things I was comfortable doing, to exploring new functions, compositions, and expressions -- but also the process of me finding out more about who I am as a designer.

Over three months of posters inevitably meant some failures, some days when I just wasn't feeling it, and a lot of posters I perhaps wouldn't display as my best pieces -- but they were all part of this project and I think they're important to include on this page. So here's the final batch, good and bad and average, an exploration of styles and techniques, and a snapshot of these past few months.
